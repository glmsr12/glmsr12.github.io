Destination unknown: What to pack for any getaway?

After years of travel journey, what I carry in my bag has drastically changed. I started my travel journey with a duffel bag first and now I travel with a big luggage and never needed anything else. Today, I want to share that knowledge with you. What should you pack on your trip?

Even though, what to pack for a trip depends on person to person and where you are traveling to, do not forget to add these on your lists!

  • Phone charger/Portable phone charger or power bank!
  • Functional water resistant backpack/fanny Pack
  • Seasonal clothing; lightweight jackets, foldable hats, flip-flops
  • Toiletteries
  • Water bottle
  • Beside your passport/cash
  • Pain killer and basic firstaid kit
